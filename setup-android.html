Android Setup Guide

Set up DOZNTV on any Android phone, tablet, or TV box.

Before you start: Make sure your device is connected to the internet and you have your DOZNTV login credentials ready from your welcome email.
1

Download IPTV Smarters Pro

Open the Google Play Store on your Android phone or tablet. Search for IPTV Smarters Pro and install it.

2

Open the app

Launch IPTV Smarters Pro. Accept any permissions it requests (storage, network).

3

Add your account

Tap Add New User then choose Login with Xtream Codes API.

4

Enter credentials

Fill in the Server URL, Username, and Password exactly as shown in your welcome email.

5

Load the playlist

Tap Add User. The app will connect and load all channels, movies, and series.

6

Browse and watch

Navigate to Live TV, Movies, or Series. Use the search bar to find any channel instantly.

Need help? Our support team is available 24/7. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through it live.
