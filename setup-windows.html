Windows PC Setup Guide

Set up DOZNTV on your Windows 10 or Windows 11 PC.

Before you start: Make sure your device is connected to the internet and you have your DOZNTV login credentials ready from your welcome email.
1

Download VLC Media Player

Go to videolan.org and download VLC (free, no registration needed). Install it.

2

Open Network Stream

Launch VLC. Click Media → Open Network Stream (or press Ctrl+N).

3

Enter your M3U URL

Paste the M3U URL from your welcome email into the Network URL field.

4

Start streaming

Click Play. VLC will load your channel list. Use Playlist view to browse all channels.

5

Alternative: IPTV Smarters for Windows

You can also install IPTV Smarters from the Microsoft Store for a better interface with EPG support and favourites.
