1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the site. Cookies cannot execute programs or deliver viruses.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential cookies

Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Session management (keeping you logged in)
  • Security tokens (CSRF protection)
  • Language preference storage
  • Cookie consent preference

Analytics cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. Data is aggregated and anonymous. We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not track you across other websites.

Functional cookies

Remember your preferences such as currency selection, language choice, and display settings to provide a more personalized experience.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Payment processors (for checkout security)
  • Analytics providers (anonymous usage data only)

We do not allow advertising networks or data brokers to set cookies on our website.

4. Your Control Over Cookies

You have several options to control cookies:

Browser settings

All modern browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may prevent the website from working correctly.

Our consent banner

When you first visit our site, a cookie consent banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage and revisiting the site.

To opt out of analytics, most analytics providers offer opt-out mechanisms or browser extensions.
