1. Overview

At [BRAND_NAME], we stand behind the quality of our service. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent. We handle all refund requests on a case-by-case basis with the goal of reaching a satisfactory resolution for every subscriber.

Because our service is digital and activation is immediate, all sales are generally final. However, we do consider refund requests in specific circumstances outlined below.

2. Eligible Situations

We will consider a refund request in the following circumstances:

  • Technical failure: The service fails to function for an extended period (more than 48 hours) and we are unable to resolve the issue
  • Activation failure: Your account credentials were never delivered or cannot be activated despite our best efforts
  • Duplicate payment: You were charged twice for the same subscription period
  • Early request: You contact us within 72 hours of subscribing before significant use of the service, and have a valid reason

3. Non-Eligible Situations

Refunds will generally not be issued for:

  • Change of mind after significant use of the service
  • Internet connection issues on the subscriber's side
  • Incompatibility with unsupported devices
  • Subscription already past 30% of its duration without a reported issue
  • Violations of our Terms of Service that led to account suspension
  • Requests made for third parties or resale subscriptions

4. How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund request:

  • Contact our support team via email or WhatsApp
  • Include your account email address and order details
  • Describe the issue clearly
  • Allow our team to attempt to resolve the issue first — many problems can be fixed quickly

We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours and provide a resolution within 5 business days.

5. Processing Time

If a refund is approved:

  • Credit/debit card refunds: 5–10 business days
  • PayPal refunds: 3–5 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: equivalent value returned in the same cryptocurrency within 5 business days

Refund timing depends on your payment provider and is outside our direct control once processed.
