How to Set Up on Amazon Fire Stick

This guide applies to Fire Stick 4K, Fire Stick 4K Max, and Fire TV Cube.

Before you start: Make sure your Fire Stick is connected to the internet and you have your subscription credentials ready.
1

Enable apps from unknown sources

Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and enable "Apps from Unknown Sources".

2

Install the Downloader app

Search for "Downloader" in the Fire TV App Store and install it. It's free and official.

3

Download the player app

Open Downloader and enter the URL provided in your welcome email. The app will download and install automatically.

4

Enter your credentials

Open the installed app and enter your username and password from the welcome email. Tap "Login".

5

Start streaming

Browse channels, open the EPG, or explore the VOD library. You're live!
