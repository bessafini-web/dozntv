iPhone & iPad Setup Guide

Set up DOZNTV on your iPhone, iPad, or Apple TV.

Before you start: Make sure your device is connected to the internet and you have your DOZNTV login credentials ready from your welcome email.
1

Download GSE Smart IPTV

Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad. Search for GSE Smart IPTV or IPTV Smarters Pro and install it.

2

Open the app

Launch GSE Smart IPTV. Tap the menu icon (≡) in the top-left corner.

3

Add a Remote Playlist

Tap Remote Playlists → Add (the + icon).

4

Enter your playlist details

Type a name (e.g. DOZNTV) and paste your M3U URL from the welcome email. Tap Add.

5

Load channels

Tap on the playlist you just added. All channels will load automatically.

6

Watch on Apple TV

To stream on Apple TV, use AirPlay from your iPhone/iPad or download IPTV Smarters directly from the Apple TV App Store.
