Smart TV Setup Guide

Set up DOZNTV on your Samsung, LG, Sony, or Philips Smart TV in minutes.

Before you start: Make sure your device is connected to the internet and you have your DOZNTV login credentials ready from your welcome email.
1

Download an IPTV player app

Open the App Store on your Smart TV. Search for IPTV Smarters Pro or Smart IPTV and install it. Both apps are free.

2

Open the app and choose M3U

Launch the app. Select Add Playlist or Add via M3U URL.

3

Enter your M3U URL

Paste the M3U link provided in your welcome email exactly as sent. Do not add extra spaces.

4

Add your EPG URL (optional)

Paste the EPG URL from your welcome email for an electronic program guide (TV schedule).

5

Save and load channels

Tap Save or Load. The channel list will load in a few seconds.

6

Log in if required

If the app asks for credentials, enter the username and password from your welcome email.

7

Enjoy streaming

Browse live channels, movies, and series. Happy watching!

Need help? Our support team is available 24/7. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through it live.
