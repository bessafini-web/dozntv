Mac Setup Guide

Set up DOZNTV on your MacBook, iMac, or Mac Mini.

Before you start: Make sure your device is connected to the internet and you have your DOZNTV login credentials ready from your welcome email.
1

Download IINA or VLC

For the best experience on Mac, download IINA (free, modern macOS player) from iina.io, or use VLC from videolan.org.

2

Open Network Stream in VLC

If using VLC: Click File → Open Network and paste your M3U URL from the welcome email.

3

Open URL in IINA

If using IINA: Click File → Open URL and paste your M3U URL.

4

Browse channels

Your full channel list will load. Use the built-in search to find channels quickly.

5

Stream on Apple TV

To watch on your TV, use AirPlay from VLC or IINA to cast to an Apple TV or AirPlay-compatible TV.

Need help? Our support team is available 24/7. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through it live.
