Amazon Fire Stick Setup Guide

Set up DOZNTV on your Amazon Fire Stick, Fire Stick 4K, or Fire TV Cube.

Before you start: Make sure your device is connected to the internet and you have your DOZNTV login credentials ready from your welcome email.
1

Enable unknown sources

Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options. Enable Apps from Unknown Sources and ADB Debugging.

2

Install the Downloader app

Go back to the Fire TV home screen. Search for Downloader in the search bar and install the free app.

3

Download IPTV Smarters

Open Downloader, type the URL we sent you in your welcome email, and hit Go. The APK will download automatically.

4

Install the APK

Once downloaded, tap Install. After installation, tap Done (you can delete the APK file to free space).

5

Enter your credentials

Open IPTV Smarters. Choose Login with Xtream Codes API. Enter the server URL, username, and password from your welcome email.

6

Load your channels

Tap Add User. The app will fetch all channels and VOD content automatically.

7

Start watching

Browse Live TV, Movies, and Series. Enjoy 18,000+ channels in HD/4K!

Need help? Our support team is available 24/7. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through it live.
