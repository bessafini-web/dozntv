Cookie Policy

This policy explains how DOZN TV uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

Last updated: January 15, 2024
What are cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall experience.

1. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie TypePurposeDuration
Essential Required for the website to function. Includes session management, security tokens, and user authentication. Session / 30 days
Analytics Help us understand visitor behavior, page views, and traffic sources to improve our website. Up to 2 years
Preferences Remember your settings such as language preference, theme, and cookie consent choice. 1 year

2. Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Session cookies: Maintain your browsing session and authentication state
  • Security cookies: Protect against cross-site request forgery and other threats
  • Load balancing: Ensure optimal server performance and content delivery

3. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This data helps us improve our content and user experience. Information collected includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or traffic source
  • Browser type, device, and screen resolution
  • Geographic location (country/city level, not precise)

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. We do not use this data to identify individual visitors.

4. Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices to provide a more personalized experience:

  • Your cookie consent preference (accept/decline)
  • Language and regional settings
  • Display preferences

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis and reporting
  • Payment Providers: Secure payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)
  • CDN Services: Content delivery optimization

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Cookie Banner: Use the cookie consent banner on our website to accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use opt-out tools provided by analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on)
Please note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website. You may not be able to access certain features or complete transactions if essential cookies are blocked.

7. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, but we respect your right to privacy and provide the cookie management options described above.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
